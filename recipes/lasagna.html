Lasagna, Morberg-style

With Google-translated English from Swedish.

It is very easy to eat too much of this, since it is so tasty.

Ingredients for the mincemeat-sauce(In Swedish!)

  • 500 g köttfärs (eller högrev att mala)
  • 100 g champinjoner
  • 100 g rökt sidfläsk
  • 400 g passerade tomater
  • 1 dl tomatpuré
  • 4 klyftor vitlök
  • 2 msk olivolja
  • salt
  • svartpeppar
  • 100 g prästost, att riva
  • 10 lasagneplattor (gröna)

Ingredients for the bechamel-sauce(Again, in Swedish. Fun, eh?)

  • 7 dl mjölk
  • 2 dl vispgrädde
  • 100 g smör
  • 1 dl vetemjöl
  • muskot, riv några drag
  • 1 dl vetemjöl
  • salt

    • Lasagna, Part 1

    1. Prepare the mincemeat
    2. Cut the pork loin and mushrooms finely.
    3. Finely chop the garlic.
    4. Fry the meat in a pan with butter until browned.
    5. Then fry the pork loin, mushrooms and garlic in a little olive oil until golden brown.
    6. Add the tomato puree.
    7. Put everything in a thick-bottomed pot (preferably an iron pot) and pour in the passed tomato.
    8. Taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
    9. Let everything simmer in the pot for 30-40 minutes(make the bechamel meanwhile why don'tcha'?).

    Bechamel

    1. Heat cream and milk.
    2. Melt the butter in another saucepan and dust over the flour.
    3. Stir with a whisk so that the flour dissolves in the butter properly.
    4. Fry the meat in a pan with butter until browned.
    5. Beat in half of the hot cream milk and stir so it is smooth and fine.
    6. Beat in the rest and whisk again.
    7. Let simmer slowly for 5-10 minutes.
    8. Smaka av med salt, peppar och några drag på muskotnöten.

    Lasagna Part 2

    1. Take a deep oven dish and place a layer of plates in the bottom.
    2. Wrap with a layer of bechamel sauce and grated cheese and a layer of minced meat-sauce.
    3. Continue until all plates are finished and finish the lasagna with a final layer of minced meat-sauce, bechamel sauce and cheese.
    4. Sätt in i ugnen och grädda din lasagne i 20-30 minuter i 200 grader.
